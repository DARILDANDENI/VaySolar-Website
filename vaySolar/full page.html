FAQs

Howmuch electricity can I generate with solar?

100 sq ft of solar panels can generate up to 1000-1400 units in a year.



How do I claim subsidies from the government?

Subsidy is only provided on PV (photovoltic) systems with Indian manufactured solar panels designed to ensure safety of the system. Due approvals are needed from the distribution company before the plant is installed.

With our vendors, you sit at home and only pay subsidized price while we work with DISCOMs for due approvals.



What equipments are needed to install solar?

Solar PV modules, Mounting Structure, Inverter, Junction Box, Data Logger, Cables & Switches. One may choose to install a battery as well.
